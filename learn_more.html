Pacific Ring of Fire

--DECEMBER 2025--

Volcanoes are located in many countries across every continent, with the highest concentration found along the Pacific Ring of Fire. The United States has the most active volcanoes overall, though Indonesia has the highest number of historically active ones. Here are some of the countries with significant volcanic activity and examples of prominent volcanoes.

Countries with the Highest Number of Volcanoes

The following countries have the most Holocene volcanoes (volcanoes that have erupted in the past 10,000 years):

  • United States
  • Japan
  • Russia
  • Indonesia
  • Chile
